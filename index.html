📖 How to Play

The Basics

  • Each week, pick one FPL player before the gameweek deadline
  • That player's FPL points for that gameweek are added to your season total
  • The player with the most points at the end of the season wins
  • If you miss a deadline, you score 0 points for that week (No Show)

Restrictions

  • You cannot pick the same player more than once per season
  • You can only pick a maximum of 3 players from the same team per season
🎮 Chips — Once Per Season Each

You have 3 chips to use strategically throughout the season. Each can only be used once, and only one chip can be played per gameweek.

🎖️
Captain
Your player's points are doubled for that gameweek. Pick your moment wisely — big fixtures, in-form strikers, or a blank double gameweek.
🃏
Wildcard
Bypasses both the "no repeat player" rule and the "max 3 per team" rule for that week. Use it to re-pick your favourite or go heavy on a team with a great fixture.
🎯
Double Delight
Pick two players — only the higher scoring one counts towards your total. A great safety net for uncertain gameweeks or injury-prone players.
